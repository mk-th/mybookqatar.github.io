Frequently Asked Questions

How does My Book work?

Printed Book: All you have to do is to choose an offer, tear it out and present it for redemption when you ask for your bill. Mobile App: You choose the offer on your smartphone, tap the offer, show it to the waiter when you ask for your bill, and follow the process to redeem it.

How many offers are available in a My Book product?

Each of our products contain more than 1400 Buy One Get One Free offers. You can view further details for our product on the product pages. Please click here to view all our offers.

Is My Book Printed book and Mobile app same?

A mobile product on My Book App contains all of the offers that are available in the printed book. However, we continue to add new merchants and special offers to My Book App throughout the year. The mobile products are also priced lower than the printed books.

Where can I purchase a My Book product?

You can purchase My Book products using our Store inside My Book App, from our office, or from one of our retailers as mentioned on our Facebook and Instagram page.
