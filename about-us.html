About Us

My Book was founded in 2012 with an intention to bridge the gap between Consumers & Merchants. The company started off in 2013 with just 80 offers with its operation and sales managed solely by the founder, Mohamed Saleh.

Today, My Book is one of the leading providers of 2 for 1 incentives in Qatar with more than 1300 offers from over 400 outlets.

My Book's commitment is to continually improve its products and provide the best services to its customers. My Book is your partner in life. After all, it has been made for you!

Join My Book and start saving today using exclusive offers for many restaurants, entertainments, spas, health & fitness, education, day-to-day services, & staycations in Qatar.
