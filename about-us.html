About My Book

My Book is a marketing platform made for clients seeking higher exposure. It was founded in 2012 with an intention to bridge the gap between Consumers & Merchants. The company started off in 2013 with just 80 offers with its operation and sales managed solely by the founder, Mohamed Saleh. Today, My Book is one of the leading providers of 2 for 1 incentives and much more in Qatar with more than offers from over outlets.

As a Qatar based company, My Book has been successful to uplift the businesses in Qatar and find a place in every resident’s heart. My Book's commitment is to continually improve its products and provide the best services to its customers. My Book is your partner in life. After all, it has been made for you!

Join My Book and start saving today using exclusive offers for many restaurants, entertainments, spas, health & fitness, education, day-to-day services, & staycations in Qatar.
