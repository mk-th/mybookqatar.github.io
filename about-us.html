About My Book

My Book is a Qatari marketing platform and a Buy1 Get1 Free incentive provider in the ME&A region founded in 2012. It was founded with the intention to bridge the gap between Consumers & Merchants. The company started off in 2013 with just 80 Buy1 Get1 Free offers. Today, My Book is one of the leading providers of 2 for 1 incentives and much more in Qatar with more than Buy1 Get1 Free offers.

In 2018, My Book Qatar was shortlisted to the final round to represent Qatar as the best national digital solution for the international World Summit Awards (WSA).

As a Qatar based company, supported by Qatari Visionaries, and with the inspiration from the wise vision of the Emir HH Sheikh Tamim bin Hamad Al Thani's Qatar National Vision 2030, My Book Qatar has been successful to uplift the various segments of Qatari businesses with its digital products. My Book Qatar's commitment is to continually improve its products and provide the best services to its customers. My Book is your partner in life. After all, it has been made for you!
